HR Manager

Location: Remote / Chennai
Job Type: Full-time
About Blyn Technologies

At Blyn Technologies, we believe that great talent drives innovation. As a leading software development company, we focus on web development, app development, and SaaS solutions. We are looking for a passionate HR Manager to help us build and support a highperforming team.

Job Description

We are seeking an HR Manager to oversee all aspects of human resources practices and processes. The ideal candidate will be responsible for hiring, employee engagement, and ensuring a productive and positive workplace culture.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement HR policies and procedures.
  • Oversee recruitment, onboarding, and employee training.
  • Manage performance reviews, payroll, and employee benefits.
  • Address employee concerns and foster a positive work environment.
  • Ensure company compliance with labor laws and best HR practices.
  • Organize team-building activities and professional development programs.
  • Maintain accurate HR records and reports.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field.
  • Proven experience in an HR role (preferably in a tech company).
  • Strong knowledge of HR best practices, labor laws, and recruitment strategies
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to handle confidential information with discretion.
  • Proficiency in HR software and tools.

Nice-to-Have Skills

  • Experience in remote hiring and HR policies for remote teams.
  • Familiarity with payroll systems and HR analytics.

Perks & Benefits

  • Competitive salary based on experience.
  • Remote-friendly work culture.
  • Opportunities for career growth and skill development.
  • Supportive and innovative work environment.
