Business Development Executive (BDE)

Location: Remote / Chennai
Job Type: Full-time / Part-time / Contract
About Blyn Technologies

Blyn Technologies is a fast-growing software development company specializing in web development, app development, and SaaS solutions. We help businesses scale with innovative and tech-driven solutions. We are looking for a dynamic and results-driven Business Development Executive (BDE) to join our team and drive growth.

Job Description

We are seeking a Business Development Executive (BDE) to generate new business opportunities, build relationships with clients, and contribute to the company’s revenue growth. The ideal candidate should have strong communication skills and a passion for sales, networking, and business strategy.

Responsibilities

  • Identify and generate new business opportunities through research, networking, and outbound strategies.
  • Develop and maintain strong relationships with potential clients and partners.
  • Pitch Blyn Technologies' services (web development, app development, SaaS solutions) to clients.
  • Understand client needs and propose customized solutions.
  • Collaborate with the marketing team to create sales strategies and campaigns.
  • Meet and exceed sales targets and revenue goals.
  • Stay updated with industry trends and competitor activities.
  • Prepare sales reports and client proposals.

Requirements

  • Proven experience in business development, sales, or client acquisition.
  • Excellent communication, negotiation, and presentation skills.
  • Strong understanding of IT services, software development, and SaaS solutions.
  • Ability to identify market opportunities and convert leads into sales.
  • Experience with CRM tools, lead generation, and cold emailing.
  • Self-motivated, goal-oriented, and able to work independently.

Nice-to-Have Skills

  • Experience in the tech industry or IT services sales.
  • Knowledge of LinkedIn outreach, B2B sales, and digital marketing.
  • Ability to analyze market trends and create business strategies.

Perks & Benefits

  • Competitive salary + performance-based incentives.
  • Flexible work hours (Remote/Hybrid options).
  • Career growth and leadership opportunities.
  • Dynamic and innovative work culture.
