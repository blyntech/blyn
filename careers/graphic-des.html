Graphic Designer

Location: Remote / Chennai
Job Type: Full-time / Part-time / Contract
About Blyn Technologies

Blyn Technologies is a cutting-edge software development company specializing in web development, app development, and SaaS solutions. We believe that great design enhances user experience and brand identity. We are looking for a creative and detailoriented Graphic Designer to join our team.

Job Description

We are seeking a talented Graphic Designer to create engaging and visually appealing designs for our digital products, marketing materials, and branding. The ideal candidate should have a passion for design, a keen eye for aesthetics, and experience in UI/UX principles.

Responsibilities

  • Design and create marketing materials, social media graphics, website assets, and branding materials.
  • Collaborate with the marketing and development teams to create UI/UX designs for web and mobile applications.
  • Develop logos, banners, illustrations, and infographics.
  • Ensure consistency in brand identity across all platforms.
  • Stay updated with design trends, tools, and best practices.
  • Optimize images and graphics for web and mobile performance.

Requirements

  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, Figma, InDesign, etc..,).
  • Strong understanding of UI/UX design principles.
  • Experience in creating web and social media graphics.
  • Ability to work with color theory, typography, and composition.
  • Attention to detail and a strong sense of creativity.
  • Ability to work independently and collaborate with a team.

Nice-to-Have Skills

  • Experience in motion graphics and video editing (After Effects, Premiere Pro).
  • Knowledge of HTML and CSS for web design implementation.
  • Understanding of branding and digital marketing strategies

Perks & Benefits

  • Competitive salary based on experience.
  • Remote-friendly work culture.
  • Creative freedom to bring ideas to life.
  • Career growth opportunities in a fast-paced environment.
