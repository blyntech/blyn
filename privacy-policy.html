Privacy Policy

Welcome to Blyn Technologies! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website, www.blyntechnologies.com, use our services, or engage with us in any other way.

We collect both personal and non-personal information in the following ways:

  1. Personal Information: Information you provide us directly, such as:
    • Contact details (name, email, phone number)
    • Feedback or inquiries submitted through forms
    • Payment information for services (if applicable)
  2. Non-Personal Information: Automatically collected information when you interact with our Site, including:
    • Device type, operating system, and browser information

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect non-personal information for a better user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

These third parties are obligated to keep your information confidential and only use it to provide the services on our behalf.

To exercise any of these rights or if you have questions about your data, please contact us using the information provided below.
